Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez’s Support Declines in Queens Despite Re-election Victory

By Mizuki Uchiyama

Nov. 08, 2024

Despite securing a fifth term, Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez's support in New York’s 14th Congressional District continues to wane. According to recent election data from the Board of Elections, AOC won her seat with 68.66% of the vote—a notable drop from her 78.2% win in 2018. Each election since has shown a gradual decline in her vote share.

“Many residents, especially those in immigrant communities, feel left out of discussions on local safety and economic stability,” said John Park, Senior Analyst on Immigrant Advocacy and Political Trends, MinKwon Center for Community Action.
